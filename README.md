# Snake Cam

A webcam-powered snake that follows your finger.

Snake Cam is a small creative coding experiment that uses your webcam to track your hand in real time. When you raise your finger, a little snake appears and follows your movement on screen. After a short delay, a red target appears. Touch the target with the snake’s head to unlock a sparkling star rain.

## Features

- Real-time webcam interaction
- hand tracking
- A snake that follows your finger
- One animated snake eye that reacts to movement
- A red target challenge
- Sparkling star rain effect
- Runs directly in the browser

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.
2. Allow camera access.
3. Raise your finger in front of the camera.
4. Move your hand to guide the snake.
5. When the red target appears, touch it with the snake’s head.
6. Enjoy the star rain.

For best results, use good lighting and keep your hand clearly visible.

## Built With

- **HTML** - page structure
- **CSS** - layout and visual styling
- **JavaScript** - interaction, animation, and game logic
- **p5.js** - canvas drawing and animation
- **MediaPipe Hands** - real-time hand tracking through the webcam

## Files

- `index.html` - the full project, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

## Privacy

Snake Cam uses your webcam in the browser for hand tracking. The camera feed is processed locally in your browser and is not uploaded by this project.
